About P.I.

Shiladitya Banerjee is a Junior Group Leader in Theoretical Physics Applied to Biology at the Department of Physics and Astronomy at University College London (UCL). He is a Fellow at the UCL Institute for the Physics of Living Systems (IPLS). Before joining UCL, Shiladitya was a Postdoctoral Fellow at James Franck Institute of the University of Chicago (2013 - 2016). Shiladitya received his PhD in Physics from Syracuse University in 2013.
